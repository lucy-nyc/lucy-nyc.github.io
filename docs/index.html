What it does

Every feature exists to make a study session faster — and every answer is grounded in the text you paste.

✦

Three question types

Multiple choice, true/false, and short answer — mix and match whatever fits the material.

⟐

You set the shape

Choose how many questions to generate and how hard they should be.

◎

Grounded in your text

Every question is answerable from what you pasted. No invented facts, no hallucinations.

⇪

Import a PDF

Pull text straight out of a lecture PDF into the material box in one click.

✓

Answer keys

Reveal or hide answers with short explanations as you work through the test.

↓

Export to PDF

Download a clean blank test, or a full answer key, ready to print and study.
