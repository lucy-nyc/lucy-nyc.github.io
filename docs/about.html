About

I'm Lucy — I like turning fuzzy ideas into shipped tools.

I'm a software engineer and web developer who cares about the whole arc of a product — from the first sketch to the moment it's live and someone is actually using it. I'm happiest building clean, focused interfaces where the design and the engineering reinforce each other.
